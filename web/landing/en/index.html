Case study
[Jewelry · Bangkok]

Three Odoo instances, consolidated into one.

A Bangkok jewelry manufacturer was running three independent Odoo instances — one per workshop — with no shared inventory, no consolidated accounting, and monthly reports built in Excel. We unified them into a single Odoo Enterprise instance, migrated three years of history, and connected the POS + Mailcow + Nextcloud stack. Cut month-end closing from 9 days to 2.
